Reddit Community Marketing
6 steps - 4 sessions learned - 89% confidence
Strategy
Target high-signal posts (10+ comments, <48h, not promotional). Write authentic replies: acknowledge problem, share experience, mention product.
Steps
1. Open Reddit, navigate to target subreddit
2. Scan posts - skip promotional, skip <10 comments
3. Open high-signal post, read top comments
4. Write reply: acknowledge → experience → mention
5. Submit, verify not auto-removed
6. Repeat for 2 more subreddits (max 5/day)
Voice & Style
casual short sentences uses emoji
Hey great point about the engagement metrics! We should def try that approach
Guardrails
Max 5 replies/day - Never identical phrasing - Never reply to own posts - Empathy-first tone
