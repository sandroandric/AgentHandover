Welcome to AgentHandover

AgentHandover is a local, privacy-first apprentice that observes your workflows and generates semantic SOPs.

  • Runs entirely on your machine — no data leaves your laptop
  • Learns from your daily work patterns
  • Generates AI-executable SOPs

This installer will set up:

  • The AgentHandover daemon and CLI tools
  • The menu bar status app
  • Chrome extension for browser observation
  • Auto-start services
